What is NODE ?

NODE, the computer club of Delhi Public School, Sector 45, Gurgaon, is one of the premier ones in the Delhi-NCR tech circuit. At NODE, we believe that our future is what governs our today; and our passion is what drives us to our tomorrow.

Today, NODE has 50+ computer enthusiasts, from classes VI - XII, each excelling in specific fields like programming, digital imaging, motion graphics, photography, video editing, app development, web development, quiz and robotics. It also organises alphanode, an annual computer symposium, in July.
